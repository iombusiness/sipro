Installing the SiPRO module is simple and follows the standard Odoo app installation process.
Before You Begin
Please ensure
your Odoo database is running on a supported version - we have tested on Odoo versions 18 and 19
the required dependent apps are installed - base, product, stock, sale, hr, contacts and purchase
you have administrator access to Apps and Settings
the module has been added to your Odoo instance by your hosting provider, technical team, or deployment partner
Install the Module
Log in to your Odoo database as an administrator.
Open the Apps menu.
Search for Inventory Risk Mitigation or SiPRO.
Click Install.

Once installation is complete, the module is activated automatically.
After Installation
After the module is installed, you will see a new main menu called Inventory Risk Mitigation.
The module also adds:
the Proposal Report
the Strategic Stock workspace
the Proposal Acceptance Log
the Strategic Stock Log
the module Settings page
the Strategic Stock Management tab on the product form
In addition, the module automatically creates a scheduled action that checks daily for expired strategic stock records and reverts them when required.

Troubleshooting
If the module does not appear in your Apps menu, this usually means:
the app has not yet been uploaded to your Odoo server
server restart may be needed
the Apps list has not been refreshed
from developer mode, try updating apps list
your user account does not have sufficient access rights
ensure you have admin previleges
It will be best to have your Odoo administrator or implementation partner activate the module.
