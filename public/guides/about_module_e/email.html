SiPRO is an advanced Odoo module built to help businesses make smarter inventory decisions by turning stock data, demand history, supplier lead times, and product cost information into practical replenishment insights.
It is designed to help businesses reduce inventory risk and improve cashflow. By providing advanced inventory analytics, forecasting tools, and automated risk assessments, SiPRO empowers your organization to make data-driven decisions and optimize stock levels across your supply chain.
Using historical stock movement data and configurable business rules, the module helps users:
•analyze product demand and usage trends
•classify inventory by demand and value
•automatically calculate safety stock and reorder point recommendations
•review proposed inventory policies before applying them
•manage exceptional or temporary strategic stock requirements
•maintain visibility through proposal and strategic stock audit logs

Requirements and Dependencies
Before installing the module, ensure the required Odoo applications and supporting master data are available in your environment.
SiPRO has been tested on Odoo 18 and 19. It may work on older versions but it is recommended to trial in a test environment prior to rolling out in production.
The module depends on the following standard Odoo modules:
  
Module
Dependency reason
base
provides the core Odoo framework, system configuration, users, companies, and general platform features required by the module.
product
provides product templates, variants, categories, costing, and unit of measure data extended by SiPRO.
stock
provides warehouses, stock moves, stock move lines, inventory quantities, and reordering rules used for analysis and replenishment calculations.
sale
supports the customer-demand context behind outbound stock activity and sales-related inventory movement.
hr
required to record the employee responsible for requesting or initiating strategic stock.
contacts
provides partner records used for vendors, customer accounts, and customer contacts.
purchase
provides supplier information and vendor lead times used in lead-time and replenishment calculations.
Operational Requirements
For the module to deliver meaningful results, the following business data should also be available and maintained correctly:
☐︎products with valid standard cost
☐︎supplier records linked to products
☐︎vendor lead times maintained on supplier information
☐︎warehouse configuration for the operating company
☐︎stock movement history that reflects real product demand
☐︎reordering rules for products where current and proposed values need to be compared
 
Recommendation
For best results, install and use SiPRO in an environment where Inventory, Purchasing, Sales, Contacts, and Employees are already part of the normal business process, and where product, supplier, and stock transaction data is actively maintained.
